Features

Spectral alchemy
in real time.

Morph, freeze, cross-synthesize. Chronos Weaver treats audio as a spectral canvas.

🔮

Spectral Morph

Real-time interpolation between two audio sources' spectral frames. Cross-fade between formants, harmonics, and noise components independently. From subtle timbre blends to alien hybrid voices.

❄️

Spectral Freeze

Capture and sustain a spectral frame indefinitely. Like a freeze pedal for your entire mix. Adjustable freeze decay, transition speed, and harmonic locking. Create infinite drones from any audio.

☁️

Granular Cloud

Generate evolving grain clouds from spectral content. Each grain inherits frequency, amplitude, and phase from the source. Control cloud density, spread, grain duration, and pitch randomization.

📊

3D Spectrum Analyzer

Interactive 3D spectrogram with rotatable view. See frequency (X), time (Y), and amplitude (Z) simultaneously. Drag to orbit, zoom for detail. Real-time FFT with configurable resolution.

🔄

Cross Synthesis

Combine spectral envelopes of two sounds. Use one sound's amplitude spectrum with another's phase/noise structure. Vocal formants on drums, percussion on pads — infinite hybrid textures.

🎛

Morph Envelope

Drawable morph envelope for automated spectral evolution over time. LFO-sync to host tempo or free-run. Smooth transitions between four morph snapshots with adjustable curve shaping.
