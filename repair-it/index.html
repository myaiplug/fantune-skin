Six spectral tools in one plugin. Point at the noise and remove it.

🌊

Spectral De-Noise

Real-time noise profiling and adaptive spectral subtraction. Learn a noise floor and remove it across the entire frequency range. Adjustable reduction amount, floor offset, and spectral smoothing.

⚡

De-Clipper

Reconstruct clipped waveforms using ML-based interpolation. Recover up to 12 dB of clipped material. Real-time with configurable reconstruction depth and transient preservation.

🔊

De-Esser

Split-band sibilance reduction with spectral detection. Frequency-specific (4–12 kHz) with adjustable threshold, range, and detection bandwidth. Preserves vocal body while removing harsh sibilance.

📊

Live Spectrogram

Real-time FFT-based spectrogram with configurable resolution, color map, and zoom. See exactly what frequencies contain noise. Adjustable FFT size (512–8192), overlap (50–90%), and window type.

🔄

Before / After

Instant A/B comparison with seamless transition. Toggle between processed and unprocessed to hear exactly what changed. The spectrogram updates live to show the difference visually.

🎯

Frequency Mask

Selective frequency masking for targeted removal. Draw a mask over any spectrogram region to remove or isolate specific sounds. Perfect for removing clicks, hums, and narrowband interference.
