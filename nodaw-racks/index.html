The Rack

Four processors. One plugin.

Drag-and-drop chain in a single VST3. Every module is a studio-grade processor.

🌀

Saturator

Analog-modeled tape and tube saturation with drive, mix, and auto-gain. From subtle warmth to crushed harmonics. Three saturation curves: Tape, Tube, Diode.

📊

7-Band Precision EQ

Low/high shelves + 5 peaking bands with variable Q. Real-time frequency response display. Surgical precision meets musical curves. 20 Hz–20 kHz range.

🌊

Spatial Processor

Stereo width, Haas delay, mid-side matrix, and spatial pan. Widen, narrow, or invert stereo field. Mono-compatible width control with correlation meter.

⚡

Reactor Limiter

Lookahead brickwall limiter with adaptive release, K-metering, and oversampling (2x/4x/8x). Transparent leveling or aggressive RMS density.

🎨

4 Hardware Skins

Obsidian leather, brushed silver, cobalt powder coat, or matte black plastic. Every skin changes the entire rack aesthetic — including knob finishes and VU colors.

💾

Full Preset System

50 factory presets by world-class engineers plus unlimited user save/load. Per-module bypass, A/B comparison, and chain drag-and-drop reorder.
