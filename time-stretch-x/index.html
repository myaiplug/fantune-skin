Algorithms

Four ways to shape time.

Each algorithm has its own character. Choose the right tool for your material.

🧊

Granular

Real-time grain synthesis with continuous control over grain size, density, shape, jitter, and envelope. Overlapping grain clouds for lush, evolving textures. Best for creative sound design and extreme stretching up to 8x.

Creative
📡

Spectral

Phase vocoder with FFT-based processing. Superior sound quality for melodic material and vocals. Configurable FFT size (1024–8192) and window overlap. Transparent stretching up to 4x with zero audible artifacts.

Transparent
🥁

Beat-Sync

Transient-aware time stretching that preserves rhythmic integrity. Real-time transient detection and preservation. Lock stretch ratio to host BPM for tempo-synced loops. Perfect for drums, percussion, and rhythmic material.

Rhythmic
📼

Tape

Varispeed analog-modeled stretching with wow, flutter, tape saturation, and stop/start transients. From subtle tape warm-up to extreme pitch bends and lo-fi warp. Includes modeled reel inertia and playback speed curves.
